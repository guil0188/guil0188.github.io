I also ended up dropping that program after the first semester, as it truly wasn't for me. In between then and now, I worked and spent 4 months in Barcelona in early 2025. I chose to go with this program because I have always been interested in learning software in Adobe and how to create a cool website, and I am positive that I will stick with this program.
